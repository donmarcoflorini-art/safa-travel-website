# Safa Travel Website

![Safa Travel Website](images/home/hero.jpg)

A custom multilingual website designed and developed for Safa Travel, a Hajj and Umrah travel company serving customers from Sweden, Denmark, and nearby European countries.

## Live Website

[Visit Safa Travel](https://safa-travel.se/)

## Project Overview

The purpose of this project was to create a clear, trustworthy, and accessible online presence for a travel company specializing in Hajj and Umrah services.

The website was designed to help visitors understand the company’s services, explore Hajj and Umrah information, and contact the company directly through WhatsApp or phone.

The visual direction focuses on clarity, trust, calmness, and accessibility rather than an overly luxurious travel style.

## Business Goals

The website was created to:

1. Present Safa Travel as a trustworthy travel company.
2. Explain Hajj and Umrah services clearly.
3. Serve Arabic, English, and Swedish-speaking visitors.
4. Make contacting the company fast and simple.
5. Provide a strong foundation for future marketing and SEO work.

## Responsive Design

The website was tested and adjusted for:

* Desktop screens
* Laptops
* Tablets
* Mobile phones

Special attention was given to navigation, language switching, text direction, image loading, and contact buttons on smaller screens.
