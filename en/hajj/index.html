The Hajj journey

Organization starts well before travel

Hajj is a great journey, and it requires careful preparation, prior knowledge, and clear communication. That is why it is important to understand the requirements and stages before choosing a program or starting registration procedures.

At Safa, we focus on clarifying the full picture for those who wish to perform Hajj from Europe: what is required? What are the stages? When should preparation begin? What should be known before making a decision?

  • Follow-up on Hajj information according to each season.
  • Clear explanation of basic requirements for travelers from Europe.
  • Explanation of registration and preparation steps.
  • Helping individuals and families understand their options.
  • Clear communication before the travel date.

What do we provide?

Practical support to understand the Hajj journey

We do not treat Hajj as an ordinary travel product. The goal is for the traveler to know what to expect, what is needed, and how to prepare in a way that respects the value of the journey.

1

Requirement explanation

We explain the basic requirements for those who wish to perform Hajj from Europe, and what to pay attention to before starting the process.

2

3

Journey preparation

4

Clear communication

We explain the next steps and help you follow important information at the right time.

Important notice Hajj details vary by season

Hajj procedures, dates, prices, registration methods, and available programs may vary from one season to another.

When the details for a specific season become available, we can explain the suitable options and required steps directly.
