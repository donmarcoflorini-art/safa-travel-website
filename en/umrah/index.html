The Umrah journey

From the idea to arrival

An Umrah journey is not just a flight and hotel booking. Travelers need to understand the program, transport, travel dates, requirements, and communication process before the journey begins.

That is why Safa focuses on simplifying every step, so the decision is clear from the beginning and the trip details are understood before registration.

  • Umrah programs for residents in Sweden and European countries.
  • Clear explanation of program details before registration.
  • Follow-up on preparations and required information.
  • Communication suitable for individuals and families.
  • Support before travel and during the journey.

Who are Umrah programs suitable for?

For individuals, families, and Muslim communities in Europe

Umrah programs are suitable for those who want to perform Umrah in an organized and clear way, whether traveling for the first time or looking for a more structured experience.

  • Individuals who wish to perform Umrah from Europe.
  • Families who need clearer organization.
  • Elderly travelers who need follow-up and prior explanation.
  • First-time travelers to Makkah and Madinah.
  • Anyone who wants to understand the details before payment and registration.
